About

Building the conditions where great work happens.

I'm a design ops leader who builds the systems, teams, and tools that help design organizations do their best work.

I'm currently a Principal Design Program Manager at Vistaprint, where I lead AI tooling strategy, prototyping infrastructure, and cross-functional programs across the design org. Before that, I built and scaled an in-house creative studio at Better, a fast-growing fintech startup, growing the team from 8 to 30 and co-leading a full brand identity launch.

Earlier in my career I spent nearly a decade as a senior producer at Publicis and Campfire, leading integrated campaigns for brands like HBO, P&G, Heineken, and L'Oreal — including three weeks on-site in Bangalore managing 100+ developers for a multi-brand platform build.

I care about the craft of making things, and about building the conditions where great work can happen consistently.
